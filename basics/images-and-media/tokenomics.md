# Tokenomics

The SYNC token is designed to capture value from the velocity of RWA movement across chains while ensuring the security and compliance of the orchestration layer.

## Core Token Fundamentals

**• Total Supply:** 1,000,000,000 SYNC

**• Token Type:** ERC-20 

**• Inflation:** 0% - fixed supply

**• Initial Circulating Supply:** 20% 

***

## Token Distribution

| Category | Allocation |

| Liquidity & MMs | 20% | 
| Ecosystem (Airdrops, Dev Grants & Bounties) | 15% |
| Community Sale | 5% | 
| Treasury | 20% | 

*** 

## Vesting & Release Schedule

**Team:** 0% unloced at TGE. 12-month cliff. Upon completion of the cliff (Month 13), a one-time unlock of 10% of the allocation will occur to align core team members with early project milestones. The remaining 90% will vest linearly over the following 36 months.

**Advisors:** 10% unlocked at TGE. 6-month cliff followed by a 24-month linear vesting

**Public Sale:** 100% unlocked at TGE.

**Treasury:** 0% unlocked at TGE. 12-month cliff followed by a vesting period over 48 months
